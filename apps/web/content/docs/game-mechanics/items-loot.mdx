
# Items & Loot

Items in TavernKeeper are procedurally generated ERC-1155 tokens with various rarities, stats, and equipment slots. Items can be found as loot, equipped to heroes, or traded on the marketplace.

## Item System

### ERC-1155 Tokens
Items are ERC-1155 tokens (Inventory contract):
- Can be minted in batches
- Can be transferred between addresses
- Have unique metadata
- Can be equipped to heroes

### Procedural Generation
Items are procedurally generated:
- Based on seeds for determinism
- Context-aware (dungeon loot, boss drops, etc.)
- Level-appropriate stats
- Class-specific options

## Item Rarities

### Common
- Basic items with standard stats
- Most common drop
- Good for early game

### Uncommon
- Slightly better than common
- Moderate drop rate
- Useful upgrades

### Rare
- Significant stat improvements
- Lower drop rate
- Valuable finds

### Epic
- Major stat boosts
- Rare drops
- Highly sought after

### Legendary
- Exceptional items
- Very rare drops
- Unique and powerful

## Item Types

### Weapons
- **Main Hand**: Swords, axes, maces, staffs, etc.
- **Off Hand**: Shields, secondary weapons, spellbooks
- **Stats**: Damage, attack speed, crit chance

### Armor
- **Head**: Helmets, hats
- **Body**: Armor, robes
- **Hands**: Gloves, gauntlets
- **Feet**: Boots, shoes
- **Stats**: Defense, resistance, bonuses

## Item Stats

### Weapon Stats
- **Damage**: Base damage output
- **Attack Speed**: How fast attacks are
- **Crit Chance**: Chance for critical hits
- **Durability**: Item condition (future)

### Armor Stats
- **Defense**: Reduces incoming damage
- **Resistance**: Reduces specific damage types
- **Bonuses**: Additional stat bonuses

## Item Modifiers

Items can have modifiers that enhance stats:
- **+X to Attribute**: Increases specific attribute
- **+X% Damage**: Increases damage output
- **+X AC**: Increases armor class
- **Special Effects**: Unique abilities

## Equipment Requirements

Items may have requirements:
- **Level Requirement**: Minimum hero level
- **Class Requirement**: Specific hero class (warrior, mage, rogue, cleric)
- **Attribute Requirement**: Minimum attribute values
- **Slot Restriction**: Can only be equipped in specific slot

## Loot System

### Sources
Items can be found from:
- **Dungeon Loot**: Treasure rooms and chests
- **Monster Drops**: Defeated monsters drop items
- **Boss Drops**: Bosses drop guaranteed rare items
- **Vendor**: Purchase from NPCs (future)
- **Quest Rewards**: Complete quests for items (future)

### Distribution
In party runs:
- Loot is distributed among party members
- Can be traded between players
- Equipped items stay with heroes

## Item Generation

### Context-Aware
Items are generated based on context:
- **Dungeon Level**: Higher levels = better items
- **Monster Type**: Different monsters drop different items
- **Boss Fights**: Bosses drop guaranteed rare/legendary items
- **Treasure Rooms**: Special rooms with better loot

### Seeded Generation
Item generation uses seeds:
- Same seed = same items
- Deterministic and verifiable
- Fair distribution

## Marketplace

Items can be traded:
- List items for sale
- Set prices in MON or KEEP
- Browse available items
- Purchase from other players
